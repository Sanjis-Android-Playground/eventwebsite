A Narrow Window for Big Outcomes

\n

Interim administrations are judged by two outcomes: stability and legitimacy. Stability requires administrative continuity; legitimacy requires visible reform and a credible path to elections.

\n
\n\n
\n

Main Constraints

\n
    \n
  • Balancing competing stakeholder expectations.
    • \n
  • Maintaining neutrality while implementing change.
    • \n
  • Economic management during uncertainty.
    • \n
  • Restoring trust in institutions.
